Be a Buddy, Not a Bully

Bullying Hurts Others...

It makes them feel sad, scared, and alone. It's like a rain cloud that won't go away.

...But It Hurts YOU, Too!

  • You lose friends. It's hard to trust someone unkind.
  • It builds a bad habit. Kindness is a skill, like soccer.
  • It feels yucky inside. Making others feel bad hurts you too.

What Can You Do?

Include others. Ask them to play. If you see something mean, tell a grown-up.
