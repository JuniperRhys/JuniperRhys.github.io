The 5-4-3-2-1 Tool

5 - SEE

Look around and name five things you can see right now. (e.g., "I see my keyboard, a cup, a plant...")

4 - FEEL

Notice four things you can feel. (e.g., "I feel the chair under me, the texture of my shirt...")

3 - HEAR

Listen for three things you can hear. (e.g., "I hear the fan, a car outside, my own breathing...")

2 - SMELL

Name two things you can smell. (e.g., "I smell coffee, soap...")

1 - TASTE

Name one thing you can taste. (e.g., "I taste toothpaste, a sip of water...")
